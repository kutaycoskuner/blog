2026-05-01 opinion

Passing judgment on matters we do not fully understand puts us in an unjustifiable position. Saying ‘I didn’t know’ or appealing to ignorance should not be accepted as an excuse in such cases.

Before reaching a judgment, one should make sure to have carefully analyzed the context and the situation as a whole, using the means available to us and putting in the necessary effort. Even if no one will hold us accountable, this matters if one value of being true.

Forming a judgment is actually very easy, and people often admire those who speak decisively because of the confidence they project. But what should be focused on is not the judgment itself, but how that judgment was reached.
